About the 3Rs

The 3Rs—Reduce, Reuse, Recycle—are the core principles of sustainable living. Together, they form a roadmap for individuals and organizations to minimize waste and promote a greener, cleaner Earth.

Reduce

Use less, waste less. Start by adopting habits like using reusable bags and avoiding single-use plastics.

Reuse

Think creatively. Reuse jars, bottles, and other containers, and repair instead of replacing.

Recycle

Give new life to materials. Recycle paper, glass, and metal, and support recycled products.
